Any external button compatible with your device can be used to trigger voice recognition. Your button must send a keyboard shortcut to IGOOR.

![[../assets/asr_button.png]]
**The default keyboard shortcut is CTRL+K**, but you can change it at any time in:

Settings > Extensions > ASR

and, in the ASR extension settings, click on the text field:

![[../assets/Pasted image 20251120124055.png]]

Then click with your external button. The key combination sent by your external button will appear in the text field.
